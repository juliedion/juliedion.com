About Me

Design That Connects,
Converts & Endures

I'm Julie Dion — a versatile, senior product designer and digital marketing leader with 18+ years transforming complex business requirements into effective design and technical solutions. My career spans Senior UI Designer at ITG for Charter Spectrum, Lead UX/UI Designer at Human Care Systems, Senior Graphic Designer at Merck Millipore, and Creative Director at Monet/Madison Handbags.

I specialize in leading cross-functional teams, building cross-platform design systems, and executing campaigns that drive measurable results — including a brand identity that generated $1.26 million in sales by Q3 and a design that earned the 2019 Graphic Design USA Health & Wellness interactive award.

Named one of the 20 Most Outstanding Women of the Capital Region (2024) and a MarCom Award winner, I bring the same passion to Fortune 500 work, growing startups, and community organizations.
