My Process

How a Page Gets Made at Charter Spectrum

Each sprint moved through a disciplined cross-functional workflow — from a Jira ticket to a live URL — covering discovery, copy, SEO review, high-fidelity design in Adobe XD, AEM authoring, and cross-browser QA.

01
Discovery & Briefing
Aligned with product, marketing, and engineering stakeholders to define promotional goals, user segments, and content priorities for each sprint.
02
Copy & SEO Review
Copywriters drafted headlines and CTAs; the SEO team reviewed keyword density, H-tag hierarchy, and meta descriptions before design began.
03
High-Fidelity UI Design
Built pixel-perfect mockups across desktop, tablet, and mobile in Adobe XD using the 200+ component Spectrum Business design system.
04
AEM Authoring & Launch
Authored final designs directly in AEM production, working alongside developers for QA across browsers and devices before each launch.
