Reinventing a Fashion Brand
for the Digital Age

Madison Handbags needed to transform from a catalog-only brand into a digitally-driven fashion retailer. The strategy centered on three pillars: a refreshed brand identity with elevated lifestyle photography, a custom online handbag configurator letting customers build their own bags, and a seasonal catalog system that worked across print and digital channels.

Design Process

Full Brand Ecosystem
in 12 Months

01
Brand Strategy
Conducted market positioning analysis and defined the Monet brand strategy — "accessible luxury" positioning that differentiated Madison Handbags in the direct-to-consumer market.
02
Visual Identity
Developed the Monet visual identity system: logo, typography, color palette, lifestyle photography art direction, and brand standards across catalog, digital, and retail.
03
Catalog & Print
Produced Fall/Winter and Spring catalogs — editorial layout, product photography curation, model shoot art direction, and print production for 2 seasonal campaigns.
04
Digital & E-Commerce
Designed the "Style All Your Own" bag builder, e-commerce site, and The Insider VIP newsletter — a loyalty program building brand community and repeat purchase.
