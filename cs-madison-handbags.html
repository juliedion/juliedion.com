The Challenge

Reinventing a Fashion Brand
for the Digital Age

Madison Handbags needed to transform from a traditional catalog-only brand into a digitally-driven fashion retailer. The Monet Handbags line had strong product quality but an identity that hadn't kept pace with the market. As Marketing and Creative Director, I owned the full scope of the brand evolution.

The strategy centered on three pillars: a refreshed brand identity with elevated lifestyle photography, a custom online handbag configurator letting customers build their own bags, and a seasonal catalog system that worked across print and digital channels.

Brand Identity E-Commerce Design Catalog Production Photo Art Direction Email / Newsletter Design
Monet Rebrand

From Catalog Brand to Fashion Identity

The Monet Handbags rebrand centered on elegance and aspiration — lifestyle photography shot in outdoor settings with model talent, a sophisticated color palette of burgundy and cream, and editorial-quality layout for the seasonal catalog. The brand's new tagline "Classy & Fabulous" set the tone for every touchpoint.

Monet Handbags — Brand & Catalog System
Digital Commerce

Custom Handbag Builder —
"Style All Your Own"

The flagship digital initiative was a custom handbag configurator — "Style All Your Own" — letting customers select from dozens of base styles, colors, and hardware options to build a personalized bag. This was a significant innovation for the brand and a primary driver of online engagement.

I designed the full UX for the configurator, product photography display, and the e-commerce site architecture that housed both the Monet line and the Madison Handbags brand.
