The Context

Designing for scientists means designing for precision.

Merck Millipore is the life science division of Merck KGaA, producing filtration systems, reagents, and lab equipment for research institutions, biopharmaceutical manufacturers, and clinical laboratories worldwide. The audience — scientists, lab directors, and procurement teams — demands accuracy, credibility, and clarity in every piece of communication.

I was brought in to produce high-impact campaign creative that could communicate complex scientific value propositions clearly and compellingly — from full-page trade journal ads to digital banners running across industry publications like BioTech Now.

My Role

Art direction and production across every channel.

I served as senior designer across the Merck Millipore account — responsible for concept development, layout design, and production-ready file delivery across print, digital, and trade show formats. Every project required a strong understanding of the Merck brand architecture and the technical nature of their product lines.

Working directly with account managers and scientific copywriters, I translated highly technical product messaging into layouts that balanced brand credibility with visual clarity — ensuring every design met both brand standards and regulatory requirements for life science communications.

Mobius Award Winner

Campaign work produced for Merck Millipore's Mobius filtration product line earned recognition in the Mobius Awards — a testament to the impact of strong creative in a highly technical, B2B space.

Print & Trade

Full-page ads and trade show creative that cut through the noise.

  • Designed full-page and spread print ads for placement in leading life science trade journals targeting research scientists and lab directors
  • Developed trade show booth and display materials for major industry conferences including ACHEMA and BIO International
  • Produced the award-winning Mobius filtration campaign banner — recognized by the Mobius Awards for creative excellence in B2B marketing
  • Created the Life Science Small Molecules (LSSM) campaign materials — translating complex chemistry into a clean, credible visual narrative

Digital & Patient

Digital ads and patient materials that inform and engage.

  • Designed digital banner ads for placement in BioTech Now and other digital life science publications — optimized for click-through and brand recall
  • Produced patient starter brochures for specialty pharmaceutical brands — balancing scientific accuracy with accessible, patient-friendly design
  • Maintained strict regulatory compliance across all patient-facing materials, coordinating with medical and legal review teams
  • Delivered production-ready files across all formats — from print-ready PDFs with correct bleeds and color profiles to optimized digital assets
