The Challenge

Building Authority
at Scale

Spectrum Business needed to establish itself as a trusted resource for small and medium-sized business owners — not just a service provider, but a partner in their growth. The Insights hub was the vehicle: a library of educational content targeting high-intent SMB search queries across technology, connectivity, security, productivity, and business operations.

The challenge was scale and coordination. Over 200 articles needed to be researched, written, SEO-optimized, designed, formatted, and published in Adobe Experience Manager — each one requiring cross-functional collaboration between copywriters, the SEO team, design, and development before going live.

My Process

From Brief to
Live Article

Each article followed a structured production workflow that moved from keyword research through design, AEM authoring, and final QA — with handoffs between four distinct teams at each stage.

01
SEO Keyword Briefing
The SEO team identified target keywords, search volume, and competitive difficulty. Each brief included a recommended article structure, H-tag hierarchy, and suggested internal links to existing Spectrum Business product pages to boost cross-page authority.
02
Copywriter Collaboration
Copywriters drafted articles against the SEO brief. I worked closely with writers to ensure content was formatted for AEM's component structure — flagging sections that needed pull quotes, callout boxes, or CTA modules as the copy was developed, not after.
03
Design & AEM Authoring
I designed and authored each article in Adobe Experience Manager — placing components, configuring metadata, adding Open Graph tags, setting canonical URLs, and embedding cross-sell CTAs that linked to relevant product pages without disrupting the reading experience.
04
Dev QA & Launch
Development reviewed structured data markup, page speed, and mobile rendering before each batch of articles went live. Post-launch, Google Search Console data was reviewed weekly to identify ranking movement and flag articles for optimization.
