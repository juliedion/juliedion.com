The Challenge

Speaking Directly to
Business Owners

Bar and restaurant owners are a distinct SMB audience with specific needs — premium sports programming, reliable TV service, and value bundles that reduce operational costs. A generic business page wasn't converting this segment. Spectrum Business needed a dedicated vertical landing page that spoke their language.

I designed the page from brief to live production: understanding the vertical's pain points, defining the content hierarchy around Sports Fan TV and the NFL Sunday Ticket promotion, and building a responsive experience that performed across every device type a hospitality owner might use.

My Process

Built for the Hospitality Vertical

01
Vertical Research
Reviewed audience data for the bar & restaurant segment — their device usage patterns, peak browsing hours, and what messaging angles drove plan inquiries from hospitality operators.
02
Content Architecture
Structured a narrative flow: hero sports bundle offer → channel lineup value → bundle comparison → social proof → CTA. Each module was designed to reduce friction for a busy business owner scanning on mobile.
03
XD Prototype
Built desktop and mobile prototypes in Adobe XD using Spectrum's design system. Iterated on the hero imagery, pricing callout design, and module layout based on stakeholder feedback.
04
AEM Production
Authored the finalized page in Adobe Experience Manager, coordinating with front-end developers on component mapping and QA'd the live page across desktop, tablet, and mobile breakpoints.
