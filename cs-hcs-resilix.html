The Work

Platform Design, Brand & Marketing Ecosystem

As Creative Director, I owned every Resilix touchpoint — from the visual brand and UX prototype to the pharma sales collateral that drove $1.26M in new program sales. The work spanned a nurse-facing web application, patient-facing microsites, print and digital marketing, infographics, and webinar design — all built within a single cohesive brand system.

Design Process

From Patient Journey to Live Platform

The Resilix design process was grounded in behavioral science — understanding that specialty therapy patients face complex, intersecting barriers at every stage of the treatment journey, from initiation through persistence.

01
Patient Experience Mapping
Collaborated with clinical and account teams to map patient barriers across initiation, adherence, and persistence. Identified key touchpoints for nurse outreach and digital communication.
02
Brand Identity — Resilix
Developed the full Resilix brand: logo, color palette, typography, and visual language. Created a system that conveyed clinical credibility while remaining warm and patient-centric.
03
UX Prototyping in Axure
Designed and iterated on the nurse-facing UI across 10+ prototype versions — nurse scheduling, patient profiles, task management, text channels, reimbursement flows, and dialer mockups.
04
Marketing Ecosystem
Produced sales one-pagers, pharma client decks, webinar presentations, microsites, infographics, and GDUSA competition materials — all within the Resilix brand system.
Platform UI

Nurse-Facing Interface Design

The Resilix platform needed to give nurse case managers an intuitive tool to manage patient communication across text, phone, and email — while tracking barriers, scheduling, and reimbursement in one view. I designed the full UI across desktop and mobile breakpoints.
