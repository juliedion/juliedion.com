The Work

Motion & Video for
Enterprise Software

Microsoft's Dynamics 365 Sell campaign required motion-first creative to reach enterprise decision-makers across social and digital channels. I designed and produced a suite of motion deliverables — each tailored to its platform while maintaining consistent messaging around the D365 three-way value proposition: sell, service, and market.

The work required balancing Microsoft's strict brand system with creative motion that would stop the scroll on Facebook and LinkedIn — a challenge that demanded both technical precision and creative boldness within tight corporate guidelines.

Process

Motion Design Process

01
Brand System Mastery
Immersed in Microsoft's strict brand motion guidelines — Fluent Design System principles, approved illustration styles, type hierarchies, and platform-specific creative specs for Facebook, LinkedIn, and web.
02
Storyboarding
Built detailed motion storyboards for each deliverable — mapping out timing, transitions, on-screen text appearance, and the narrative arc that each format would carry within its 15–30 second window.
03
Motion Production
Produced final motion in After Effects and Premiere — sound-off optimized for social autoplay, with caption-level copy integrated into the motion rather than relying on audio to convey the message.
04
Format Optimization
Delivered each asset in the precise technical specifications required by Microsoft's trafficking team — resolution, codec, bitrate, aspect ratio, and safe zone compliance for each platform.
